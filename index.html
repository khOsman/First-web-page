Independent Universities, Bangladesh

Independent University, Bangladesh or IUB is a university in Bangladesh. Its permanent campus is in Bashundhara R/A, Dhaka.Its business school has received Ameriacn accreditation from ACBSP. The university was established in 1993 by the Private University Act, 1992.

North South university

North South University is the second non-government university of Dhaka, Bangladesh. Its Business School is also the first in Bangladesh to receive American accreditation from ACBSP.

Brac University

BRAC University is a private University in Bangladesh. It was founded by Fazle Hasan Abed in 2001 under the Private University Act, 1991.
